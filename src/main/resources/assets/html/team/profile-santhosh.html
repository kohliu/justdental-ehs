Dr. Santhosh Kumar

CEO - JUST DENTAL

Dr Santhosh is the Founder and CEO of JUST DENTAL – a venture of the Just Dental Health Care Pvt Ltd.

His key responsibilities include managing operations and scaling up the business across India. He is also a member of the company’s Board of Directors.

A dentist by qualification. Santhosh has been in the Pharma – life sciences industry for almost 15 years. Prior to Just Dental, Santhosh was leading the QuintilesIMS Data management team across India and South Africa. Between 2003 to 2007, Santhosh held several positions in Accenture and TCS.
