Dr. Venkatesh Garla

Dr Venkatesh Garla was awarded masters in Orthodontics in 2006 and M.Orth from the Royal College of Surgeons, Edinburgh,U.K. in 2007.

He is extensively involved in teaching and training future Orthodontists and annually supervises a number of M.D.S research projects. He is also an author of many peer reviewed articles within the Orthodontic literature. He has lectured in many Orthodontic forums across the country.

At JUST DENTAL, Dr Venkatesh manages our Learning & Development and supports the Marketing team.
